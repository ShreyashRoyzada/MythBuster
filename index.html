Are you the quicker one?
Do you know the myths and facts around?



Challenge your pal, and find out!
Only the smartest and most agile will prevail.

We fight myths, spread awareness in a fun manner!

You can play it alone or team up with a friend! Steps to play is very simple, and as follows:

  • Click on "Click to Commence"
  • Two balls of different colours will float across the screen.
  • The faster one of you two, clicks the ball of their colour first :P
  • the faster one gets to answer a question, i.e. whetjer the statement that popped up is a myth or a fact!
  • If correct, the more agile person wins!

The game is just the tip of the iceberg. While we're definitely willing to build a newer and innovative versions of the game itself, following are some other future implementations we see!


Susceptibility Analysis

How susceptible is the player to fake news? Are their any particular areas of interest where user tends to believe in myths? An analysis of the same can be made possible and can help users to be socially aware.

Greater Awareness

Such games open dialogue for sometimes deep-rooted myths by virtue of the spontaenity of the game. Players can discuss, learn and enjoy all at once!

Response Time

Fastest Finger First! This game isn't just a wake up call for the myths you believe in, but also your response time! The fastest person wins!!

Being Social

Overall it makes you a better social animal by making you socially aware, while encouraging social interaction by playing the game!
